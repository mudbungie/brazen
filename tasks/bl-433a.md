Downstream (lernie) persists brazen's canonical vocabulary verbatim: transcripts and response.json are JSONL of v=1 events, and archived workspaces must replay bit-identical years later. Today nothing in brazen's test suite pins the *serialized* shape of the canonical request/event vocabulary — a serde change (rename, tag layout, skip_serializing_if) would ship as an ordinary release and silently break every persisted downstream transcript. Ask: checked-in raw JSON golden fixtures covering every CanonicalRequest / Event / CanonicalError shape, deserialized and round-tripped in tests, so any change to the v=1 serialization fails the suite and forces an explicit EVENT_SCHEMA_VERSION bump. Origin: lernie's brazen-seam review, 2026-07-16.

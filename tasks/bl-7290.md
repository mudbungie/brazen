The full auth capability: the Auth trait and ApiKey/Bearer/OAuth2 impls; HeaderSpec data on the provider row vs auth-mode-dependent headers on the Auth impl; the Cred enum and CredStore (XDG paths, 0600, one-file-per-provider, atomic temp+rename); the pure OAuth functions (build_authorize_url with PKCE S256, parse_callback CSRF, build_token_exchange_request over the Grant enum, parse_token_response with absolute expires_at, is_expired query); silent in-band refresh through the Transport seam; and the bz login control-plane verb with Device flow (RFC 8628 interval/slow_down/expiry) and AuthCode+loopback flow (RFC 8252 literal 127.0.0.1:0, BrowserLauncher/CodeReceiver injection). Defines the offline test strategy for the whole flow.
